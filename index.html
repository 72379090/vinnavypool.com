Overview

Swimming is a suitable exercise for people of all ages. It can help a person get or stay in shape, and the benefits also extend to mental health. It is important to incorporate strength training exercises into any routine to keep the muscles strong and flexible. As a person’s health permits, they should continue this for as long as possible throughout life. People who are 19–64 years of age should aim to get 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity each week. Younger children should be physically active as well.

Benefits Of Swimming

  • It provides a whole body workout(muscles)
  • It is suitable for all ages and fitness levels
  • It is easy on the joints
  • It is good for people with injuries(knee pains)
  • It helps regulate blood sugar
  • Increses better sleep
  • it provides self protection incase of water floods
  • Recreational Fun and Sports
